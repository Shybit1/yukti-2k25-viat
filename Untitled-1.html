About YUKTI

YUKTI 2K25 is a premier techno-cultural fest that brings together the brightest minds and creative talents from across the state. With a perfect blend of technical competitions, cultural performances, workshops, and interactive sessions, YUKTI offers a platform for students to showcase their skills, learn from experts, and network with peers.


Join us for two days of innovation, creativity, and celebration as we push the boundaries of technology and culture. Whether you're a tech enthusiast, an artist, or simply curious about the future, YUKTI 2K25 has something for everyone.
